  • Philosophy・Vision

Dstyle group. statement

“We continue to pursue true harmony between the beauty, health, and abundance of mind and body.
We will improve the well-being of society through many encounters.”

The very existence of each and every member of Dstyle group embodies this philosophy. We define “Dstyle Benefit” as “beauty, health, and abundance of mind and body.”

mission

“We will serve as a guide for each person to
achieve their own aspirations and become the
best partner on that journey.”

To this end, we will act as follows. We will deepen the harmony of “Dstyle Benefit” and share its value, joy, and encounters with everyone. We will continue to conduct research by collaborating with academia, science, and medicine and utilizing big data to foster true “Dstyle Benefits.”
Through the most effective and valuable products, methods, and services, we will continue to provide a ``place’’ that supports each person in realizing the ``self they want to become.’’

vision

“Walk gracefully, passionately and enjoying the path to beauty.”

In order to realize the “Dstyle Benefit,” we
incorporate cutting-edge technology, knowledge,
and values, and innovate with innovative data
analysis and scientific approaches. By continuing to
take on challenges with sincerity, we will open the
door to a bright future, and together with many
people, we will walk through the endless possibilities
that lie ahead.

value

  • Creating strong bonds
    We foster bonds as a group and continue to grow together through friendly competition.
  • cutting edge progress
    We constantly research “Dstyle Benefits” and “ideal encounters” and continue to stay on the cutting edge of the times.
  • limitless data power
    We will continue to collect all kinds of data in order to realize "Dstyle Benefit" and "encounter."
  • Creating new value
    We will continue to innovate through research through collaboration with academia, science, and medicine, aiming for a better future.
  • Passion and joy for challenges
    In all of our activities, we will continue to take on challenges without forgetting the passion and joy of accomplishing them.
  • Truthfulness and initiative
    We will pursue only the truth based on facts and data, and will have a positive influence on those around us by taking the initiative and setting a good example.
  • Investment in Miku
    We believe that today’s actions will create tomorrow’s “Dstyle Benefit” and a better society and happiness, and we will make generous efforts and investments in Miku.
  • magic of encounter
    Through all of our businesses, we provide “places” for new encounters and create magical and wonderful experiences and spaces.
  • Altruism and graceful passion
    We value altruism and share joy gracefully and passionately with all.
  • honesty and trust
    We comply with ethics and compliance, build integrity and trust, and contribute to respecting diversity and realizing a sustainable society.
